# Phonology

## Sound Inventory
Consonants:
- `χ` - /x/.
- `τ` - /t/.
- `λ` - /l/.
- `ζ` - /ɕ/.
- `κ` - /k/.
- `ς` - /s/.
- `π` - /p/.
- `δ` - /v/.
- `η` - /ŋ/.
- `ν` - /n/.
- `ρ` - /r/ or /ɾ/.
- `β` - /b/.
- `ξ` - /j/.
- `μ` - /m/.
- Between adjacent vowels - /ʔ/.
- `!` - /ǀ/, /ǀɛ/ when at the start of a word.

Vowels:
- `ε` - /ɛ/.
- `α` - /ə/.
- `ο` - /o/.
- `ι` - /i/.
- `γ` - /ʊ/.
